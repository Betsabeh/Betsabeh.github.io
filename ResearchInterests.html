Research Interests

I am passionate about exploring various facets of artificial intelligence and its applications. My primary research interests include:

  • Machine Learning: Developing algorithms that allow computers to learn from and make predictions based on data.
  • Deep Learning: Investigating advanced neural network architectures to solve complex problems.
  • Computational Biology: Applying computational techniques to understand biological processes and systems.
  • Health Care and Biomedical Applications: Leveraging AI to improve health outcomes and enhance biomedical research.
  • Image Processing: Utilizing algorithms to analyze and manipulate images for various applications in science and medicine.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Exploring innovative AI methodologies to address real-world challenges.

Currently, I am focused on cancer survival prediction using multimodal data and deep learning techniques, aiming to contribute to more accurate prognostic models in healthcare.
